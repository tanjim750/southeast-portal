Merit based scholarship will remain valid for 3 consecutive semesters (2 semesters for Pharmacy & LLB) from the begining. In the 4th semester (for Pharmacy & LLB 3rd semester), students will be awarded scholarship based on CGPA of previous 3 semesters (2 semeseters for Pharmacy & LLB). From 5th (for Pharmacy & LLB 4th) semester and onwards students will be awarded scholarship based on TGPA.
Student admitted with 40% / 60% scholarship may be eligible for a lower category of scholarship based on TGPA in the scholarship chart but nobody will be eligible for a higher category of scholarship based on TGPA.
Once a student decides to claim a merit waiver based on their semester resuits, they will not be allowed to move back to scholarship based on their HSC and SSC results.
