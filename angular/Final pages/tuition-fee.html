About Tuition & Fees

Summer tuition rates are approved at the Board of Trustees meeting in February before the term begins. Course registration dates can be found on the . Required Documents for Admission
  • Admission Form on payment of Tk. 500/-
  • Attested copies of all academic certificates and mark sheets (1-8 for diploma students) along with original documents
  • 4 Copies of recent photographs (passport size), Student’s NID/Birth certificate and Guardian’s NID photocopy attested
  • Attested Job experience certificate (only for 48 credits MBA)
  • Foreign Students: A copy of recent passport (at least one (1) year validity) Copies of all academic documents together with a testimonial, attested by the Diplomatic Mission Stating the duration of the courses studied by the applicant. Police Clearance Certificate from own country. "NOC from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs & Ministry of Education of the Government of Bangladesh.
